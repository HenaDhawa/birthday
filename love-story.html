Our Story

The moments I keep replaying.

  1. ✦
    The Beginning

    The Day We Met

    I still remember our first awkward conversation on Aisle—two awkward people trying their best not to disappoint each other. 😊
    And I still remember you saying, "Don't call me 'ji'... I'm not that old!" That line still makes me smile.

  2. ✦
    First Spark

    Our First Conversation

    Hours slipped by like minutes. Somewhere between the laughter, the teasing, and your terrible-but-lovable jokes, I realized I wanted more conversations just like this with you.

  3. ✦
    Official

    The Day You Became Buro

    I don't know when it happened, but I got completely addicted to talking to you. Somehow, our conversations became the best part of my day.Even when you drive me crazy or make me mad!!

  4. ✦
    Adventure

    Our First Trip Together

    Old places somehow felt brand new with you. That trip taught me that travel isn't about the destination—it's about who you're sharing the journey with. Every moment was better because you were beside me. But next time, please walk a little slower, buro. ❤️

  5. ✦
    Milestone

    Through the Hard Days Too

    Love isn't just about the beautiful moments—it's about who stays through the hard ones. I love how you somehow always know when I'm sad or upset, even before I say anything.And no matter what, you never fail to cheer me up... even if it's with your terrible jokes. ❤️

  6. ✦
    Today

    Right Now, Loving You

    Still choosing you. Still smiling at my phone like an idiot when you text. Still counting down to the next time I get to see your face. Happy birthday, GOLU❤️.
